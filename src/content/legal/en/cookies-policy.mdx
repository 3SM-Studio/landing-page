# Cookie Policy

_Last updated: 24.03.2026_

## 1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on a user's device when using a website. They may be used to ensure the proper functioning of the website, increase security, remember settings and support selected external features.

## 2. Whether we use cookies

Yes, our website may use cookies or similar technologies to the extent necessary for its operation, security and the proper display of content.

As of the last update of this policy, **we do not use our own analytics or marketing tools requiring a separate cookie consent mechanism**.

## 3. What types of cookies may be used

### a) Necessary cookies

These are files needed for the proper functioning of the website, its security, stability and the correct loading of core resources.

Without these files, the website may not function properly.

### b) Functional and technical cookies of external providers

We use external services on the website which may use their own cookies or similar technologies if the user interacts with them or when their resources are loaded by the browser.

This applies in particular to:

- **Google Maps** - for displaying the embedded map,
- **Google Fonts** - for the correct display of fonts.

## 4. External services

We use the following external services on the website, which may involve the processing of technical data or the use of their own technologies on the provider's side:

- **Google Maps**,
- **Google Fonts**,
- hosting infrastructure by **Vercel**.

Detailed rules regarding how these tools operate and any use of data by them are set out in the privacy policies of their providers.

## 5. Consent to cookies

If in the future we implement additional analytics, advertising or marketing tools using cookies or similar technologies on the website, they will be activated only after obtaining the appropriate user consent - if such consent is required by applicable law.

This does not apply to technologies necessary for the functioning of the website.

## 6. How to manage cookies

You can manage cookies at the level of your web browser, in particular by:

- blocking their storage,
- deleting stored files,
- limiting the operation of third-party cookies.

Restricting or blocking certain cookies may result in some website functions not working properly, in particular elements embedded from external services.

## 7. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy, especially in the event of technological changes, the implementation of new services, changes in the way the website operates or changes in the law.

The current version of the policy is always published on this page.
