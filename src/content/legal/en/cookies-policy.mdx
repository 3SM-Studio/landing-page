# Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are and how we use them on the website [website address].

## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on the user's device while using a website. They may be necessary for the operation of the website or used for analytical, functional or marketing purposes.

## 2. What cookies we use

### a) Necessary cookies
These cookies are used for the proper operation of the website, security, session handling and remembering basic settings, such as the website language. These cookies are necessary for the service to function.

### b) Analytical cookies
If we enable analytics tools such as Google Analytics, these cookies help us understand how users interact with the website. They will only be activated after the user's consent has been obtained.

### c) Marketing cookies
If we enable marketing tools such as Meta Pixel, these cookies may be used to personalize ads and measure campaign effectiveness. They will only be activated after the user's consent has been obtained.

## 3. Legal basis for using cookies

We use necessary cookies on the basis of our legitimate interest in ensuring the operation and security of the website.

We use analytical, functional and marketing cookies only on the basis of the user's consent. Consent must be informed, voluntary and expressed through an active action. We do not use pre-ticked consent boxes.

## 4. Managing cookies

Users can manage cookie settings through the consent banner available on the website, as well as through their browser settings.

Restricting necessary cookies may affect the proper functioning of the website.

We may use third-party services on the website that apply their own cookies or similar technologies, including in particular:

The scope and operation of these tools depend on the providers of those services.

## 6. Changes to this Cookies Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy if the way the website operates, the tools we use or the applicable laws change. The current version is always published on this page.
