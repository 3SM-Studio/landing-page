## 2. What data we process

In connection with the use of the website, we may process:
- data provided through the contact form, in particular name, email address, phone number and message content,
- technical data related to the use of the website, such as IP address, device information, browser information and language settings,
- information stored in cookies, in accordance with this Cookies Policy.

## 3. Purposes and legal bases for processing

We process personal data for the following purposes:

### a) Handling contact form submissions and replying to messages
The legal basis is Article 6(1)(f) GDPR, meaning our legitimate interest in handling correspondence and responding to inquiries, and where the contact relates to taking steps before entering into a contract - Article 6(1)(b) GDPR.

### b) Establishing, pursuing or defending legal claims
The legal basis is Article 6(1)(f) GDPR, meaning our legitimate interest.

### c) Ensuring the proper operation of the website
With regard to necessary technical data and strictly necessary cookies, the legal basis is our legitimate interest in ensuring the security and proper operation of the website. For cookies other than necessary cookies, the legal basis is the user's consent.

## 4. Is providing data mandatory?

Providing data in the contact form is voluntary, but failure to provide it may make it impossible for us to respond.

## 5. Data retention period

We store contact form data for the time necessary to handle the message and then for the period necessary to protect against or pursue possible legal claims.

Data related to cookies is stored for the period specified in the settings of those cookies or until consent is withdrawn, where consent is the legal basis.
